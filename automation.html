Common questions

The things people usually
ask before they start.

Do I need to understand any of this to use it?

No. You describe the problem in plain English. I handle the rest. The only thing you'll interact with is the weekly brief in your inbox — and that's a three-minute read, not a dashboard.

What if I already have a website?

We can work with it. The chatbot and automations can sit on top of an existing site. If the site itself is the problem, we talk about whether a rebuild makes sense — but there's no pressure. Start with whatever piece is most urgent.

How long does setup take?

One conversation to get the brief. Five to ten working days for the build. You review it, we adjust anything, and it goes live. After that, you don't need to do anything — it runs.

What if I want to change something later?

Tell me in plain English. I describe it to the agent. It gets updated. That's the same process on day one as it is on day three hundred. You're never stuck with what we built on the first pass.

Can I get a grant for this?

Possibly. The LEO Trading Online Voucher covers up to €2,500 of digital marketing and e-commerce costs — matched 50/50. I can walk you through whether your project qualifies. Ask me when we chat.

Is there a contract or lock-in?

No lock-in. Month-to-month after the setup. Free trial before the first charge — you only pay once you've seen it working. If you want to stop, you stop. No awkward conversations.

What trades and industries do you work with?

Mainly Irish trades and sole traders — plumbers, electricians, heating engineers, builders, HVAC. Also accountants, consultants, and small service businesses. If you're under 50 people and the founder is the bottleneck, this works for you.
